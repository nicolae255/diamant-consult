Frequently asked questions

Good to know before contacting us.

What does a private sale cost exactly?+

2% of the final sale price, all in. This covers the initial consultation, valuation, marketing, photos, listings, viewings, negotiation, the final report and support through to signing at the notary. No hidden fees.

What's included in the €600 notary viewing pack?+

The pack covers 2 to 3 weekends of open-house sessions for a public auction, the pre-visit property inspection, explaining the Biddit process to buyers, a detailed report after each session and a final summary.

Do you operate outside Brussels?+

Yes. We cover all 19 Brussels municipalities and certain areas of Wallonia (Walloon Brabant, Waterloo, La Hulpe, Wavre). For other locations, get in touch — it's worth a conversation depending on your project.

How quickly will I hear back?+

We respond to all messages within 48 hours. For an immediate response, calling +32 471 85 39 25 is the fastest route — including evenings and weekends.
