Method · Private sale

From first contact / to signing.

Six steps. One point of contact. You keep control of the decisions, we handle the execution — and it all concludes with a detailed report.

01

Initial consultation

On-site visit, property analysis and price estimation. We define the sales strategy together.

02

Our notary takes over

We introduce you to our notary. They handle the legal framework and official communication — offers come directly to you.

03

Marketing & promotion

Professional photos, written listing, distributed on Immoweb, Logic-Immo and our partner networks.

04

Scheduling & viewings

We handle all enquiries, schedule slots and run viewings — evenings and weekends included.

05

Offers & your decision

Every offer received is sent directly to our notary. All decisions and any follow-up go exclusively through them.

06

Signing & final report

Our notary finalises the deed. We hand you a detailed report: visitors, offers received and market analysis.
